================================================================================
FORMATTING & STYLING
================================================================================

Background Colors:
  • Header sections (Rows 1-12):  Light blue (#d9e1f2) 
  • Form labels (2,3,4,5,7,9):    Light gray (#e7e6e6)
  • Checkbox rows (6,8,10):       Very light gray (#f2f2f2)
  • Data cells (14-15):           White (#ffffff)

Text Properties:
  • Font: Calibri, 11pt
  • Headers: Bold
  • Instruction text: 10pt
  • Cell padding: 6px
  • Border: 1px solid black on all cells

Special Characters:
  • "✔" (Checkbox mark) used in delivery mode, modalities, and assessment rows
  • Appears 3 times in the form section

Expandable Structure:
  • Rows 14-15 are template data rows
  • Users can copy/paste these rows to add more modules
  • No fixed limit to number of data rows
